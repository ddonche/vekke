Perfect Siege

By Intl. Vekke Council ~ 4 min read

Another kind of siege is the 8-sided, or Perfect siege. In this siege, a unit becomes completely surrounded on all 8 sides (note that this results in immediate capture).

Capture on Completion

Since a July Siege (7-sided) is only 1 space away from a Perfect Siege, which surrounds a unit on all 8 sides, it is important to note that perfectly sieged units become automatically captured.

You can see here the July and the highlighted token, which will be moving along route 7/2 to form the Perfect.

July siege in place; next move (route 7/2) completes the Perfect

Once the eighth unit moves into position, Turtle's unit is captured.

Strategy
If you do not want the token captured, do not move beyond a July.

Perfectly sieged unit is automatically captured

Edge Units

You cannot siege an edge unit with a Perfect. Units on the edge of the board cannot be sieged by more than 5 tokens, as there are only 5 adjacent spaces. This means an Ikeda is the biggest siege you can do on an edge unit. Below you see the Kraken unit on f5 under siege this way.

Edge siege limit (maximum 5-sided Ikeda on f5)
